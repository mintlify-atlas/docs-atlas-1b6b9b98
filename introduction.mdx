## What is Casa de Historias?

Casa de Historias (House of Stories) is an Android mobile application dedicated to preserving and celebrating the rich oral traditions of indigenous communities in the Sierra de Zongolica region of Mexico. The app provides a digital platform for recording, sharing, and discovering legends in both Spanish and Nahuatl languages.

<Note>
This app serves as a cultural bridge, helping preserve indigenous stories for future generations while making them accessible to a wider audience.
</Note>

## Why Casa de Historias?

The indigenous communities of Sierra de Zongolica have a wealth of oral traditions that risk being lost over time. Casa de Historias addresses this by:

## Key Concepts

### Stories (Leyendas)

At the heart of the app are the stories themselves. Each story in Casa de Historias includes:

- **Bilingual titles and content** - Spanish and Nahuatl versions
- **Narrator information** - Who told the story
- **Community attribution** - Which village or area the story comes from
- **Optional audio** - Original recordings in the narrator's voice
- **Optional images** - Visual elements to enhance the story
- **Geographic coordinates** - Where the story takes place or originated

### User Experience

The app provides multiple ways to discover and interact with stories:

**Explorar (Discover)** - Browse curated collections, newest stories, and stories with audio

**Leyendas (Story List)** - View all available stories in a comprehensive list

**Mapa (Story Map)** - Explore stories geographically using an interactive map

**Favoritos (Favorites)** - Save and revisit your favorite stories

**Perfil (Profile)** - Manage your contributions and account settings

### Data Storage

Casa de Historias uses a hybrid data storage approach:
