Casa de Historias requires an account to contribute stories and save your favorites. The app provides a simple authentication flow to get you started.

## Creating an Account

To create a new account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap 'Regístrate' (Sign Up)">
    From the login screen, tap the "Regístrate" link at the bottom.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your information">
    Fill in the required fields:
    - **Full Name** (Nombre completo) - Your display name
    - **Email** (Correo electrónico) - Must be a valid email address
    - **Community of origin** (Comunidad de origen) - Optional field to share your community
    - **Password** (Contraseña) - Minimum 6 characters
    - **Confirm Password** - Must match your password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap 'Crear Cuenta'">
    Once all required fields are complete, tap the "Crear Cuenta" button to create your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The community field is optional but helps connect storytellers from the same region.
</Note>

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 6 characters
- Must match in both password fields
- Use the eye icon to show/hide your password while typing

## Signing In

If you already have an account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type the email address you used to register.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your password">
    Type your password. Use the visibility toggle icon to see what you're typing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap 'Iniciar Sesión'">
    Tap the "Iniciar Sesión" button to sign in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Error Messages

The app validates your input and shows helpful error messages:

| Error | What it means |
|-------|---------------|
| "El correo es obligatorio" | Email field is required |
| "Ingresa un correo válido" | Email format is invalid |
| "La contraseña es obligatoria" | Password field is required |
| "Mínimo 6 caracteres" | Password is too short |
| "Las contraseñas no coinciden" | Passwords don't match (sign up) |

<Warning>
  If you see an error message after submitting, read it carefully - it will explain what needs to be corrected.
</Warning>

## Loading States

While the app processes your request, the login button shows a loading spinner. The button is disabled during this time to prevent duplicate submissions.
