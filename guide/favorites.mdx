The Favorites feature lets you create a personal collection of legends that resonate with you. Stories you favorite are saved to your account for quick access.

## Adding Stories to Favorites

You can mark any story as a favorite from the story detail screen.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a story">
    Tap any story card to view the full story detail.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap the heart icon">
    In the top-right toolbar, tap the heart icon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="See the confirmation">
    The heart fills in with color to confirm the story is favorited.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Heart Icon States

- **Outline heart** (♡) - Story is not in favorites
- **Filled heart** (♥) - Story is saved to favorites
- **Color**: Filled hearts appear in red (FloresCeremoniales color)

<Note>
  Your favorites are tied to your account, so they sync across sessions.
</Note>

## Removing from Favorites

To unfavorite a story:

1. Open the story detail screen
2. Tap the filled heart icon in the toolbar
3. The heart returns to outline style, confirming removal

<Card title="Toggle Behavior" icon="rotate">
  The favorite button is a toggle - tap it once to add, tap again to remove.
</Card>

## Viewing Your Favorites

Access your favorites collection from the bottom navigation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Favorites">
    Tap the "Favoritos" or "Mis Favoritos" tab in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse your collection">
    Scroll through your saved stories. They appear in the same card format as the main story list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap to read">
    Tap any favorite story to open it in the detail view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Favorites Screen Layout

The Favorites screen displays:

- **Top bar**: "Mis Favoritos" heading in bold
- **Story cards**: Same design as the main list with:
  - Cover image
  - Titles in Spanish and Náhuatl
  - Narrator name and community
  - Audio indicator (if applicable)
- **Scrollable list**: Vertical scrolling with 16dp spacing between cards

### Empty State

If you haven't favorited any stories yet, you'll see:

- Heart emoji (❤️)
- Heading: "Aún no tienes favoritos"
- Message: "Explora las historias y marca tus favoritas con el corazón"

This friendly prompt encourages you to start building your collection.

## Loading States

When opening the Favorites screen:
- A loading spinner appears while fetching your favorites
- Once loaded, the story cards appear

<Note>
  Favorites load from your account each time you open the screen to ensure you see the most up-to-date collection.
</Note>

## How Favorites Work

The app tracks favorites using:
- Your user account ID
- Story IDs you've marked as favorite
- Real-time synchronization with the backend

When you favorite a story:
1. The app saves the favorite to your account
2. The heart icon updates immediately
3. The story appears in your Favorites screen

When you unfavorite a story:
1. The app removes it from your favorites
2. The heart icon clears immediately
3. The story is removed from your Favorites screen (refresh to see the change)

## Use Cases for Favorites

<Card title="Why Use Favorites?" icon="star">
  - **Remember stories** - Save legends you want to revisit later
  - **Share with others** - Build a collection to share with family or students
  - **Cultural preservation** - Curate stories from your community
  - **Learning** - Bookmark stories with Náhuatl text for language study
  - **Quick access** - Find your favorite legends without searching
</Card>

## Tips

- Favorite stories as you read them to build your collection naturally
- Use favorites to mark stories you want to share with others later
- Return to your favorites to re-read cherished legends
- Combine favorites with the map to plan visits to story locations

<Warning>
  Favorites are personal to your account. If you sign out or use a different account, you'll see a different favorites collection.
</Warning>
